What is it?

It is considered cyberbullying when someone threatens, humiliates , targets or harass another person online. This can be done through many websites and apps.

Statistics

According to studies , instagram is the social media with more worldwide cyberbullying due to the fact that it is very popular and people usually comment. Additionally, UNICEF proved that children and teenager are the ones who suffer the most.

Consequences

During and after being a victim of cyberbullying, the person can suffer from anxiety, depression, dependence of vices and other conditions that affect their mental health. If the problem increases significantly, the victim may commit suicide.

How to prevent it?

If you are victim of cyberbullying you should report and block the cyberbully, as well as talking with an adult that can manage the situation. You should also speak with a psycologist to stabilize your mental health.
